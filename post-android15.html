Android 15: What's New and Why It Matters

Gabe Mobile 5 min read

A breakdown of the most significant changes in Android 15, focusing on privacy enhancements, battery life improvements, and the new features for foldable devices.

Focus on Privacy and Performance

Android 15 continues Google's commitment to user privacy, introducing new API restrictions on background app access and better control over health data. Performance gains are subtle but noticeable, especially in power efficiency due to deeper system optimizations.

"The biggest change isn't a flashy feature, but the foundational improvements that give users more control over their data and battery life."

Key Feature Highlights

  • Partial Screen Sharing: Share only a specific app window, not the whole screen.
  • Enhanced Battery Saver: New extreme mode for significantly extending device uptime.
  • Satellite Connectivity API: Standardized support for emergency satellite messaging.
